Print & Advertising

VOLT Streetwear

A circuit-board graphic print built for a streetwear label's 'system failure' capsule drop.

ClientVOLT
ServicePrint & Advertising
Timeline4 Business Days
SoftwareIllustrator, Photoshop
Overview

VOLT's 'Offline Error' capsule needed a print that looked like a screen glitch translated onto fabric — technical, slightly chaotic, but still wearable.

Client Brief

The brief asked for a front-of-shirt graphic combining circuit-board linework with bold, glitch-adjacent typography, designed to sit on an oversized, washed-black tee.

Goal & Challenge

The challenge was keeping a dense, technical illustration legible on fabric at actual wearing distance, where fine linework can disappear into the weave of a garment.

Research & Process

I built the circuit pattern in modular sections, thickened every line beyond what would normally be needed on paper, and tested the print at actual garment scale before finalising placement around the type.

The Solution

The finished print spreads asymmetric circuit paths across the chest, broken by bold 'OFFLINE ERROR' and 'SYSTEM FAILURE' lockups in acid green and white, with the VOLT wordmark anchoring the bottom right.
