Packaging Design

Aqua Pure

A clean, trust-first label and packaging system for a mineral water brand — designed to read as pure at a glance, on a shelf or in the hand.

ClientAqua Pure
ServicePackaging Design
Timeline4 Business Days
SoftwareIllustrator, Photoshop
Overview

Aqua Pure needed a label and full bottle packaging that communicates one thing instantly — purity — without leaning on the generic blue-wave clichés that crowd the bottled water aisle.

Client Brief

The brief asked for a droplet-led mark, a clean wave motif, and mandatory compliance icons — halal certification, recycling, and responsible disposal — integrated without cluttering the label.

Goal & Challenge

The challenge was designing one label that had to work twice over: flat and legible as printed artwork, and still clear and trustworthy once wrapped around a curved, semi-transparent bottle in real-world lighting.

Research & Process

I designed the label flat first — droplet mark, wordmark, and certification icons — then mocked it onto the bottle in Photoshop against a natural rock-and-water setting to pressure-test legibility and colour under realistic conditions.

The Solution

The finished label uses a single blue droplet rising out of a horizontal wave, with the wordmark set directly into the water line so the whole mark reads as one continuous motion rather than a badge sitting on top of a background.
