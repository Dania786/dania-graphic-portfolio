Bleu de Chanel OOH

A conceptual out-of-home billboard campaign exploring how a fragrance's mood can be told through light rather than product shots alone.

ClientConceptual Project
ServicePrint & Advertising
Timeline5 Business Days
SoftwarePhotoshop, Illustrator
Overview

This self-directed project explored what a large-format fragrance advertisement could look like at night, when a billboard has to compete with a city's own lighting rather than daylight.

Client Brief

The concept centred on 'timeless elegance' — a tagline built around confidence rather than ingredients, translated into a scene that felt cinematic rather than catalogue-like.

Goal & Challenge

The challenge was making the bottle feel like it belonged inside the scene, not pasted on top of it — the light, shadow, and reflections all needed to behave as if the product were physically there.

Research & Process

I built the composition around a mountain silhouette and a glowing moon, wrapping a single ribbon of neon light around the bottle to tie the product to the landscape. Two lighting passes were produced — a deep midnight version and a softer dusk version — to compare mood.

The Solution

The final billboard sits the bottle at the centre of a neon-lit night scene, with the ribbon of colour connecting the cap to the base like a signature. Mounted on a mock storefront, it reads instantly even from across a busy street.
