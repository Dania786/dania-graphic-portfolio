About Me

Every brand has a story. Good design makes sure people remember it.

I'm Dania Hasnain, a graphic designer focused on creating visual identities that are clear, consistent, and built with purpose. My work includes brand identity, logo design, social media design, print design, and portfolio website design.

I believe design should solve problems, not create distractions. That's why I pay close attention to structure, typography, color, and the details that help a brand present itself with confidence.

As I continue to grow as a designer, I'm committed to creating work that reflects quality, thoughtful execution, and a genuine understanding of visual communication.

Process

What You Can Expect

A calm, considered process from first message to final files — every step handled with the same care as the design itself.

Strategy-First Thinking

Every design decision is tied back to your audience, your goals, and how your brand needs to be perceived.

Professional Communication

Clear updates, honest timelines, and a process that keeps you informed at every stage of the project.

Creative Problem Solving

When the obvious solution doesn't fit the brief, I look for the one that actually solves the problem.

Attention to Detail

Alignment, spacing, and colour consistency are checked and rechecked before anything is called finished.

Print-Ready Production Files

Consistent Brand Identity

Design Philosophy

Design should communicate before it impresses.

A strong visual identity isn't built through trends or effects. It's built through clarity, consistency, and thoughtful decisions.

I approach every project with the belief that good design should make ideas easier to understand and brands easier to remember. Every choice—from typography and color to layout and composition—is made with purpose.

When design communicates clearly, it doesn't need to ask for attention. It naturally earns it.

CommunicationDesign should make your business easier to understand, not just easier to look at.
TrustConsistency across every touchpoint is what turns a first impression into a returning customer.
GrowthA brand system should be built to scale with a business, not need rebuilding every year.
